Frequently Asked Questions

Q What is overstock pro?

A OverstockPro is a new Overstock shopping experience tailored exclusively to meet the needs of our business and professional customers.

Q What features are available in OverstockPro Beta? Why should I use it now?

A You will be assigned a dedicated concierge

You get an exclusive 15% off discount code on every full price item everyday (on overstock.com)

Free shipping on any order over $45 everyday

If you ever need to return any items you will receive a discount on the return shipping

Q How and when will I receive my discount?

A After you sign up and have been approved, our concierge will contact you within 1 business day to confirm your account activate your discount on all future purchases.

Q I haven’t been contacted by the concierge. What should I do?

A Please allow 1 business day for the concierge to contact you. If you haven’t been contacted after 2 business days, please email overstockpro@overstock.com or call (800) 273-6063 Monday – Friday from 9 AM – 5 PM MST.

Q When will the full OverstockPro platform be ready ? What features will that have?

A We are planning on being fully functional by early 2019. We plan to provide you an entire shopping experience from product search to order placement along with dashboards and way more features tailored for your needs.

Q Can I make purchases with a PO?

A Yes! To set up PO purchasing you must contact the Concierge at overstockpro@overstock.com or call (800) 273-6063 Monday – Friday from 9 AM – 5 PM MST.

Q How do I invite my team/coworkers to OverstockPro Beta?

A Currently the Beta doesn’t support teams/ companies as a single group. This will be supported in a future update.

Q I can’t find the products I am looking for? What do I do?

A If you can’t find the products you are looking for please contact the Concierge and they will find the items you need and customize your order to your liking (overstockpro@overstock.com or call (800) 273-6063 Monday – Friday from 9 AM – 5 PM MST).

Q How do I get help?

A Please contact us via email at overstockpro@overstock.com or call (800) 273-6063 Monday – Friday from 9 AM – 5 PM MST.

Whether you have questions about joining the OverstockPro program, or need assistance in finding the right product, or have a project that we can research and bid for you, we are here to help you. We are your Concierge.
